Privacy Policy

1. Data Controller

This service operates as a local community tool for Barking Riverside.

2. Lawful Basis and Consent

Under the UK GDPR, our lawful basis for processing your personal data is Consent (Article 6(1)(a)).

3. Data Collected & Purpose

We collect your Email, Full Name, Postcode, Phone (optional), and Address strictly to automate the submission of the official GOV.UK environmental reporting form on your behalf.

4. Third-Party Transmission

Whenever you submit or join a smell report, your personal data is programmatically transmitted to GOV.UK to file that specific report on your behalf, and then deleted. If you additionally opt into data pooling, you grant standing consent for us to also transmit your personal data to GOV.UK automatically whenever other Barking Riverside residents log a new smell event, without you needing to submit or join each time.

5. Data Retention

Your data is retained indefinitely to enable continuous automated reporting until you explicitly withdraw your consent.

6. Right to Erasure

You have the right to request the deletion of your data at any time. You can do so by visiting the Manage My Data page.
